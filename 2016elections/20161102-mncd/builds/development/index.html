The Race for Congressional District 1

Rep. Tim Walz (D)

Minnesota's 1st Congressional District is represented by Democrat Tim Walz, who has served 5 terms in office.

He is running against Republican Jim Hagedorn.

The district has a median income of $57,321 and has a minority population of 8 percent, compared to 19% of the state as a whole. About 16 percent of the district's residents are age 65 and older, compared to 14 percent statewide. Roughly 27 percent have bachelor's degrees or higher, compared to about 33 percent throughout Minnesota.

The district is Barely Republican with a PVI of R+1. In 2012, the district backed Barack Obama (D) for president by about a 1-point margin.

Open Seat
